Who am I?


Hello! I'm Smrithi, a conversation designer and user experience designer. My passion for the fields began to blossom during my undergraduate studies, thanks to a Systems Analysis course and 2 internships. From there, I pursued a master's degree, further building my expertise. Over the course of my professional journey, I've dedicated myself to honing my skills in both fields allowing me to explore the intersection of these disciplines.


As a hobby, I enjoy reading of comic books. It's my creative escape and a constant source of inspiration. This hobby not only gives me a break from my work but also fuels my creativity, influencing my approach to design.


Thank you for taking the time to get to know a bit about me. I'm excited to connect with you!
