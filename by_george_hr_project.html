Employee Protection App

Mobile App UI

Company: By George HR


Project Context

My supervisor wanted to create an all-in-one app that provided information about employment laws specifically focused on sexual harassment and allowed people to report any sexual harrassment incidences anonymously.

Problem
There weren’t enough resources that had an all-in-one solution to tackle several different issues. Resources on employment laws were scattered in many places and there were very few apps focused on sexual harassment in the workplace.
Solution
Create a mobile app that covered functions to help protect employees.
Design Process
Survey
At the beginning of this project, I reached out to 28 people, who were currently working and were over the age of 18, and asked them the following types of questions.
  • The apps they generally used
  • Their demographics
  • The type of companies they worked for
  • Their knowledge about laws surrounding harassment
  • How open and easy it is for employees to communicate their thoughts
  • How important prevention is at their company
  • How often they thought about sexual harassment
    • Here were some of the findings:
  • Users worked for companies that placed some emphasis on employment protection. Many companies had some open communication policies with their employees
  • Most users worked in many different industries but generally used apps that pertained to their daily life
  • Almost all users had little or some knowledge about laws surrounding sexual harassment laws
  • Most users don’t think about harassment often
    • Competitive Analysis
    After conducting the survey, I created a competitive analysis on different apps that focused on sexual harassment prevention. Each app approached the awareness of sexual harassment differently. Some apps allowed people to report incidents while other apps focused on promoting awareness. Also, some apps were made outside of the United States

    Use Case Diagram

    After creating the personas, I went on to create a use case diagram. This was done to figure out how to accommodate all users. There were a few goals to consider. First, the app had to be anonymous. All users had to be protected from being tracked. They also needed the freedom to talk about different situations openly. Second, the app had to have a communication feature for human resources advisors. My supervisor wanted to provide free advice by having a few human resource advisors on standby. Finally, there had to be a way to explain state and national laws

    Discussion

    There were a few points to note throughout the design process. The survey respondents were friends and family members of my colleagues. Their friends and family might have been biased when responding to my survey. Also, I did not consider employment apps as a part of the competitor research. I solely focused on apps that were focused on sexual harassment prevention. Also, it is unknown whether sexual harassment prevention laws have changed in the last one to two years. The changes were not reflected on the app.

    Lessons, Takeaways and Follow Up Tasks

    This was the first time I went through the UX design process and I learned many things. First, I learned to iterate and reiterate my designs for this project. I was never done with designing the high-fidelity mockups. There was always something that needed to be tweaked to help make the design better. Secondly, complexity and ambiguity can cause confusion. My main goal was to make sure that user’s understood the purpose of different pages on the app. I needed to make sure that everything had a clear intent.


    Finally, I learned that design and experience can mix with one another. I was so focused on the function of the app that I did not recognize the need for design until after my usability tests. User interface design and user experience design can intersect one another depending on the situation. It is important to recognize and remember that it is based on the user’s preferences when deciding whether to focus on the experience over the design and vice versa.


    The next step for this project is to conduct another round of user testing. Since this app only had one round of testing, there could be aspects within the design that might have been missed. There is also the fact that certain laws if there had been any changes to them in the last 1 to 2 years.
